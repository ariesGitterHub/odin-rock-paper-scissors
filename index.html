Wu Xing (五行) Roshambo

Rules of Elemental Combat:

  1. Choose an ancient element.

  2. Next, your opponent (the computer) will choose an element.

  3. When both selections are revealed, the stronger element choice will determine the winner of that round, and will receive one point. (Ties will be replayed until a winner is determined.)

  4. The match champion will emerge after winning five points.

Click "OK" to begin a new
